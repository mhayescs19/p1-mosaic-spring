p1-Mosaic Project - Remake of StudentVUE and ParentVUE

This project was created over the last 6 weeks of trimester 2. Our site hosts both a Student and Teacher interfaces that feature home, grades, individual and school information pages. Our project is powered by an Amazon's EC2 and uses the Dynamo Databasing service from Amazon Web Services. The user is able to view live grades from 5 classes and view individual assignments within. We also have included miscellaneous AP FRQ labs from AP Classroom activities, as well as other introductory labs to which demonstrated our early learnings with Spring, HTML and CSS.
